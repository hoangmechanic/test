    Quiz instructions

    The ISO9001:2015 FY2020 E-learning is opened to all RVC employees.

    The purpose of this training is: (i) introduce the background of ISO Standard (fundamental concepts, principals, components); (ii) perform the process 7.3 Awareness (ensure the awareness of people in organization on quality related information/data) in RVC.

    The training period is from Apr/01 to 5:00PM of May 31, 2020.

    There are 5 questions in the quiz. Each question has from 4 to 5 answer options. You must answer correctly all 5 questions to pass the quiz. During the training period, you may retry the quiz with unlimited of times until you pass the quiz.

    If you have concerns or need support, please contact Ms. Phan Thi Hoang Yen (IT Dept.) and/or Mr. Pham Tuong Hai (QAT Dept.).

    Thank you for your participation into the training.
